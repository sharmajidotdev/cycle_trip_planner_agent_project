# Example Prompts

- **Simple route setup:**  
  "Plan a 4-day cycling trip from Porto to Lisbon at ~80 km per day. I prefer budget hostels."

- **Weather-sensitive request:**  
  "Cycle from Vancouver to Seattle over 3 days, 70 km per day. Avoid heavy rain if possible and suggest indoor stops."

- **Elevation-aware trip:**  
  "Ride from Geneva to Annecy in 2 days at 60 km/day. Flag any hard climbs and propose easier alternates."

- **Budget-focused plan:**  
  "Plan 5 days from Copenhagen to Aarhus, 90 km/day. Keep lodging around 90 EUR/night and give a total budget."

- **POI-rich weekend:**  
  "Two-day loop starting and ending in Girona, 65 km/day. Include scenic viewpoints and a museum stop each day."

- **Visa clarification:**  
  "I'm a US passport holder cycling from Bilbao to Biarritz over 3 days. Do I need a visa and can you add costs?"

- **Adjustments after a draft:**  
  "Great plan. Can you shorten it to 3 days and add a note to stay near riverside paths where possible?"
